## 1. Relationship to Paddle

Because Paddle acts as Merchant of Record for all Subscription purchases, Paddle's own refund and chargeback mechanics apply to the payment transaction itself. This Policy describes MUSHIN's own position on when a refund is appropriate; where the two differ, Paddle's actual processing capability governs what's practically possible. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: confirm this Policy's stated refund windows/conditions are things Paddle can actually execute as MoR before publishing — a refund policy that promises something the payment processor can't technically do is worse than no policy.`

## 2. Subscription Fees

Monthly and annual Subscription fees are non-refundable except: (a) where required by applicable law in Customer's jurisdiction; (b) at MUSHIN's discretion for demonstrated service failure materially preventing use of core features; or (c) within [14] days of an initial paid Subscription (first-time purchase only), on request. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: statutory cooling-off/refund rights differ by customer jurisdiction (notably EU/UK consumer-adjacent protections, less relevant for genuine B2B but worth confirming Customer is always a business entity in practice) — confirm the 14-day figure and conditions against actual applicable law rather than treating it as a safe default.`

## 3. Credits

Credits reserved for a job that fails to complete due to a Service-side error are automatically released and not charged. Credits consumed by a successfully completed job are not refundable. Unused credits at the end of a billing period do not carry over unless stated otherwise in Customer's specific plan. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: confirm this matches the actual reservation/ledger behavior (ADR-026, ADR-030) rather than describing an idealized version of it — the credit-cancellation interaction (ADR-030) is still pending sign-off, so any customer-facing language here should wait for that to finalize.`

## 4. Cancellations

Customer may cancel at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period unless immediate cancellation is requested, in which case no pro-rated refund is provided for the remainder of the period except as required by law.

## 5. Chargebacks

Customer agrees to contact MUSHIN before initiating a chargeback with their card issuer. Unwarranted chargebacks may result in Service suspension pending resolution.
