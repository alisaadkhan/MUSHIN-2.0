# MUSHIN Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

## 1. Scope — Important Distinction

This DPA governs MUSHIN's processing of **Customer Data** (as defined in the Terms of Service) on Customer's behalf, where **Customer is the controller and MUSHIN is the processor**, consistent with GDPR Article 28 and equivalent frameworks (UK GDPR, and analogous obligations under applicable law).

**This DPA does not cover Creator Data.** For Creator Data, MUSHIN acts as an **independent controller** — MUSHIN, not Customer, determines the purposes and means of collecting and processing that data. Creator Data processing is governed by the Privacy Policy and Creator Data Usage Policy, not by Customer's instructions under this DPA. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: this distinction is legally significant and enterprise customers' legal teams will likely probe it — be prepared to explain clearly why Creator Data isn't processed "on Customer's instructions" the way Customer Data is.`

## 2. Definitions

Terms not defined here (Controller, Processor, Personal Data, Data Subject, Processing) have the meanings given in GDPR, incorporated for defined-term consistency regardless of which specific law applies to a given transfer.

## 3. Processing Details (Annex I)

- **Subject matter:** provision of the Service.
- **Duration:** for the term of the Subscription, plus the post-termination retention window in the Privacy Policy.
- **Nature and purpose:** hosting, storage, and processing of Customer Data to provide Service functionality (workspace tools, campaign management, analytics on Customer's own data).
- **Categories of data subjects:** Customer's own personnel/users of the Workspace.
- **Categories of personal data:** names, work emails, login/usage data, and any personal data Customer chooses to include in Customer Data (e.g., notes referencing individuals).

## 4. MUSHIN's Obligations

MUSHIN will: process Customer Data only on documented instructions (including as set out in the Terms); ensure personnel are subject to confidentiality obligations; implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (Annex III / Security Policy); assist Customer with data subject rights requests relating to Customer Data; notify Customer without undue delay of a personal data breach affecting Customer Data; and delete or return Customer Data at the end of the relationship, per Customer's election.

## 5. Subprocessors (Annex II)

MUSHIN uses the subprocessors listed in the Subprocessor List, which MUSHIN maintains and updates. Customer will be notified of new subprocessors with [30] days' notice and may object on reasonable data-protection grounds. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: confirm the actual notice mechanism (email list, in-app, a hosted changelog page) before this is contractually binding — this needs to be a real, operational process, not just a promise.`

## 6. International Transfers

Where Customer Data is transferred outside the EEA/UK, MUSHIN will rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (or the UK Addendum, as applicable) or another valid transfer mechanism. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: the actual SCC execution with each subprocessor needs to exist before this clause is more than aspirational — treat this as a real compliance task, not a documentation task.`

## 7. Security Measures (Annex III)

Summarized here, detailed in the Security Policy: workspace-level isolation enforced at the database layer, encryption of sensitive credentials at rest, access controls and audit logging for staff access to Customer Data. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: as with the Privacy/Security Policy, this Annex should describe current operational reality, confirmed at time of publication, not the architecturally-intended target state.`

## 8. Audit Rights

Customer may request evidence of MUSHIN's compliance with this DPA (e.g., relevant certifications once obtained) no more than once per 12 months, or following a confirmed security incident. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: enterprise customers frequently want a right to conduct or commission an actual audit, not just request documentation — decide MUSHIN's position on this before it's negotiated deal-by-deal.`

## 9. Liability

Liability under this DPA is subject to the limitation of liability in the Terms of Service. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: some enterprise counterparties require DPA-specific liability carve-outs (uncapped liability for data protection breaches specifically) — a template cross-reference to the general Terms cap may not survive negotiation.`

## 10. Term

This DPA remains in effect for as long as MUSHIN processes Customer Data on Customer's behalf.
