## 1. If You're a Creator Reading This

You are not a MUSHIN customer, and you did not sign up for MUSHIN. MUSHIN is a tool used by agencies and brands to find and evaluate public creator profiles for marketing purposes. This page explains what information we have about you, why, and how to request changes.

## 2. What We Collect

Publicly available information from your Instagram, TikTok, and/or YouTube profiles: your profile details, follower/engagement statistics, and content metadata. Where you've made contact information public on your profile (an email address, a WhatsApp number, a business website), we may index that too. We also generate our own analysis of your public profile: an estimated authenticity score, audience quality signals, and a content-category classification. Full technical detail on how these are generated is in the AI Usage Disclosure.

## 3. Why We Collect It

To provide a discovery and evaluation tool for brands and agencies looking to work with creators — similar in purpose to how a talent directory or media kit aggregator works, but built from public information rather than creator-submitted profiles.

## 4. Legal Basis

We rely on **legitimate interests** as the legal basis for this processing under GDPR and similar frameworks: the information is already public, our interest in operating this kind of tool is a legitimate business purpose, and we don't process special categories of data (health, religion, political opinion, etc.) or use your information in ways likely to cause you unjustified harm. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: this section reflects a not-yet-finalized Legitimate Interest Assessment — do not treat this framing as final. If you disagree with this balancing conclusion for your specific information, use the objection right in Section 6.`

## 5. If You Are a Minor / Believe You May Be Flagged as One

If our systems detect signals suggesting a profile may belong to someone under 18 — a stated age, platform child-directed content settings, or similar — we apply additional restrictions by default: your profile is excluded from paid outreach and campaign features regardless of what any MUSHIN customer's subscription includes. This is a safety default, not a complete policy — if you are a minor and have concerns about your information appearing in the Service at all, contact us using Section 7 and we will treat it as a priority request. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: MUSHIN's underlying product policy on minors is still being finalized — this section describes the current technical safeguard accurately, but should be revisited once that policy decision is made, and may need to become more protective, not less.`

## 6. Your Rights

You can request that we:
- **Tell you** what information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Delete** your information from the Service
- **Object** to our processing, including our legitimate-interest basis for it

Deletion requests are honored by removing your information from active use; to prevent it from simply being re-collected the next time your public profile is indexed, we retain a minimal marker (not your personal data itself) recording that you've opted out. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: confirm this describes the actual tombstone/erasure mechanism accurately.`

## 7. Not Used to Train Third-Party AI

Your information is used to power MUSHIN's own search and analysis features and is not sent to third-party AI companies to train their general-purpose models. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: same standing flag as elsewhere in this document set — confirm this is actually true of MUSHIN's own model-improvement practices too, and adjust if not.`
