## 1. Purpose

This Policy sets out prohibited uses of the Service, in addition to the Terms of Service. Violation may result in suspension or termination.

## 2. Prohibited Uses

Customer must not:

1. **Resell, republish, or redistribute** Creator Data or MUSHIN-derived analytics outside the Service, or use them to build a competing dataset or product.
2. **Scrape, reverse-engineer, or bulk-export** the Service beyond documented export features and rate limits.
3. Use the Service to **harass, stalk, discriminate against, defame, or make adverse decisions** about a Creator inconsistent with applicable law (e.g., using Creator Data as the basis for unlawful discrimination in a hiring-adjacent or credit-adjacent decision — the Service is not designed or warranted for such use).
4. Use the Service in a manner that **violates the terms of service of the underlying platforms** (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) in a way that creates legal exposure for MUSHIN or other Customers.
5. **Circumvent rate limits, credit metering, or workspace isolation** controls, or attempt to access another Customer's Workspace.
6. Use the Service to **contact, evaluate, or take commercial action regarding a Creator profile flagged by the Service as likely belonging to a minor**, beyond what the Service's own default protections permit — this restriction cannot be disabled by Customer regardless of plan tier.
7. Conduct **security testing, penetration testing, or vulnerability scanning** against the Service without prior written authorization from MUSHIN.
8. Use the Service for any **unlawful purpose**, or in violation of export control or sanctions law applicable to either party.
9. Introduce **malicious code** or attempt to disrupt the Service's availability for other Customers.

## 3. Reporting Violations

Suspected violations, including by other Customers, can be reported to [ABUSE/SUPPORT EMAIL].

## 4. Enforcement

MUSHIN may investigate suspected violations, suspend access pending investigation, and terminate accounts for confirmed violations, per the Terms of Service. `REVIEW_REQUIRED: due-process expectations (notice, opportunity to respond) before suspension vary by customer jurisdiction and contract value — a template one-size response may not be appropriate for enterprise accounts with negotiated MSAs.`
