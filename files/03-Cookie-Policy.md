## 2. Legal Basis for Cookie Use

`REVIEW_REQUIRED — this entire section needs jurisdiction-specific counsel input, since cookie consent law is genuinely more fragmented than most other privacy topics: PECR (UK) and the ePrivacy Directive (EU) require opt-in consent for non-essential cookies before they're set, not just a disclosure; this is a different (and stricter) standard than CCPA's opt-out model. A cookie consent banner mechanism needs to actually be built to match whichever standard is chosen — this policy should describe what's actually implemented, not what's aspirational.`
