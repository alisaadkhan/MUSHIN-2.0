## 1. Who This Policy Covers

This Policy describes how MUSHIN handles two distinct categories of personal data, under two different legal frameworks:

- **Customer personal data** — information about individuals at Customer organizations (names, work emails, login activity) who use the Service. MUSHIN is the **processor** for this data; Customer is the **controller**. Full terms in the Data Processing Agreement.
- **Creator data** — publicly available information about Creators indexed by the Service. MUSHIN is the **independent controller** for this data; Customers are not the controller of it, and simply access it as a feature of the Service. This distinction matters: it means MUSHIN, not the Customer, is legally responsible for the lawfulness of collecting and processing Creator data, and Creators should direct rights requests to MUSHIN directly (Section 8).

## 2. Creator Data: What We Collect and Why

**What:** publicly available profile information, engagement metrics, and content metadata from Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube; contact details (email, WhatsApp number, business website) where publicly listed; MUSHIN-derived analytics (authenticity score, audience quality estimate, niche classification, cross-platform identity matches). Full detail in the Creator Data Usage Policy.

**Legal basis:** legitimate interests (the relevant basis under GDPR Article 6(1)(f) and equivalent provisions elsewhere), on the basis that: the information is already public; MUSHIN's interest in operating a discovery/analytics service for legitimate B2B marketing purposes is a real one; and the processing does not involve special-category data or produce legal or similarly significant effects on the Creator that would require a different basis. `REVIEW_REQUIRED — do not treat this paragraph as settled: MUSHIN has an open, not-yet-completed Legitimate Interest Assessment. This section should be finalized against that memo's actual conclusions, not drafted ahead of them. If the LIA concludes a different basis is needed for any category (e.g., contact information specifically), this section needs to change accordingly.`

**Automated decision-making:** Creator scores and classifications are generated by MUSHIN's AI/ML systems (see AI Usage Disclosure). `REVIEW_REQUIRED: assess whether these scores could be considered to produce "legal or similarly significant effects" on a Creator under GDPR Article 22 (e.g., if a low authenticity score materially affects a Creator's ability to secure brand deals) — if so, additional safeguards (human review rights, explanation rights) may be required beyond what's currently described here.`

**Not used for model training:** Creator data is used to power MUSHIN's own scoring and search systems and is not shared with third-party AI providers to train their general-purpose models. `REVIEW_REQUIRED — same flag as in the Terms of Service: confirm whether Creator Data is used to retrain MUSHIN's own internal models before finalizing this sentence, since it needs to state the true practice.`

## 3. Customer Personal Data: What We Collect and Why

Account and billing information (name, work email, billing details — the latter primarily held by Paddle as Merchant of Record, not MUSHIN directly), usage and log data (login activity, feature usage, IP address, device/browser information), and any content Customer's users create within the Service (Customer Data, as defined in the Terms).

**Legal basis:** performance of the contract with Customer (providing the Service), and legitimate interest in securing and improving the Service.

## 4. How We Share Information
